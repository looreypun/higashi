About Us

Higashi FC is a dynamic football club based in Chiyo, Hakata Ward, Fukuoka. Our mission is to promote the growth of football in the region and provide a platform for talented players to showcase their skills.

Our Philosophy

At Higashi FC, we believe in the power of unity, discipline, and continuous improvement. Through dedicated training, strategic gameplay, and a never-give-up attitude, we aim to leave a lasting impact in the world of football.

Team

Meet our exceptional team of Managers, players, and support staff who make Higashi FC thrive. From our experienced managers to the dedicated players who wear our colors with pride, we are a family united by a shared passion for the game
